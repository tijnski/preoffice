PreOffice Suite - AI-Powered Office Extension by Presearch

PreOffice brings the power of AI directly into your LibreOffice workflow with PrePanda, your intelligent writing assistant.

Features:
- Ask PrePanda: Chat with AI about your document, ask questions, get suggestions
- Summarize Selection: Instantly summarize selected text
- Improve Writing: Enhance your writing style and clarity
- Translate: Translate text to any language
- Explain Selection: Get explanations of complex text
- Proofread: Check for grammar, spelling, and style issues

Integration:
- Menu bar integration under "PrePanda" menu
- Toolbar buttons for quick access
- Right-click context menu for selected text
- Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+P to open PrePanda

Privacy:
- Your documents stay on your device
- Only selected text is sent when you explicitly request AI assistance
- Powered by Venice.ai for privacy-preserving AI

For more information, visit https://presearch.com/preoffice
